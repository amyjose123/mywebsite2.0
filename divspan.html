Creative at heart, techie by choice, adaptable always

Hello! I'm a second-year B.Tech Computer Science Engineering student at Vellore Institute of Technology, Vellore. While I spend most of my time wrestling with code and debugging my way through life, my passions extend far beyond the keyboard. Growing up, I mastered the graceful moves of Bharatnatyam and Mohiniyattam and dabbled in piano—though let’s just say my fingers are now more familiar with typing than playing melodies. These experiences have shaped my creative outlook, even if my dancing now involves dodging deadlines rather than choreographed steps. Alongside my creative side, I dabbled in the ancient art of Abacus, completing three levels and picking up some nifty mental math skills along the way.

When I’m not convincing my code to behave, I unwind by watching movies, listening to music, sketching, and spending quality time with family and friends. I also love getting lost in cooking videos or watching influencers work their makeup and skincare magic. If only my acne would cooperate like that! These activities recharge me and spark fresh ideas—because let’s be real, inspiration often strikes while binge-watching your favorite series. I like to think I’m a unique blend: part logical coder, part free-spirited artist, and someone who believes a good laugh (and maybe snacks) can solve most of life’s challenges.

I believe in learning by doing, embracing every new challenge, and finding joy in the unexpected. Whether it’s cracking a tough problem, sketching something out of the blue, or bonding over shared memes, I approach life with adaptability and humor. My dream? To excel in tech, infuse creativity into every project, and maybe, just maybe, turn late-night ideas into something extraordinary. At heart, I’m a loyal friend, an avid learner, and someone who believes that the best adventures are the unplanned ones.
